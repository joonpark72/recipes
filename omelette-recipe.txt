1. Get a frying-pan ready, preferably 9-12 inches.
2. crack 3 eggs onto the slightly heated pan.
3. use a spatula to gently fold the eggs continuously over low-med heat.
4. stop when it has the right consistency.
5. you can always throw in some chopped veggies or meats but not necessary.
6. toss it onto a plate and eat it with your favorite condiment.
